More about offside

A player is in an offside position if: he is nearer to his opponents' goal line than both the ball and the second last opponent. An offensive or attacking player can't be ahead of the ball and involved in the play unless there is a defender between him and the goalkeeper.

Any other leagues?

World is made of 6 governing football federation bodies. Africa (CAF), Asia (AFC), Europe (UEFA), America (CONCACAF), South America (CONMEBOL), Oceania (OFC). Each governing bodies host their own leagues based on countries. Below is the list of few well-known leagues to follow.

  • K-League - Korea
  • J-League - Japan
  • AFC Champions League - Asia
  • Super League - China
